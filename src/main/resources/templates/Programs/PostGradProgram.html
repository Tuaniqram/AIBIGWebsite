Post-Graduate Programs


AIBIG welcomes suitably qualified applicants for postgraduate study (Ph.D and Master by Research). We offers students for pursuing their study in the AIBIG’s research areas. Our supervision ecosystems is designed to facilitate discussion through expert supervision, presentations in reading and writing groups and masterclasses, as well as participation in the AIBIG’s research seminars led by distinguished scholars and writers. Financial assistance is available to all doctoral candidates to support traveland publication expenses.

For more detail visit Jabatan Sains Data


POSTGRADUATE OPPORTUNITY IN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Are you interested in pursuing postgraduate studies in Artificial Intelligence? We have exciting research opportunities available in the field of AI at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Big Data (AIBIG), located at UMK Kampus Kota, Kota Bharu Kelantan. Join our collaborative team and contribute to cutting-edge projects in Generative AI, Big Data, and Cancer Bioinformatics.

  • One position for Master by Research(Computing) - 1.5 to 2 years
  • One position for PhD by Research (Computing) - 2.5 to 3 years

    • Research Areas:
    1. Artificial Intelligence (Generative AI)
    2. Big Data
    3. Cancer Bioinformatics
    Requirements:
    1. Malaysian citizenship
    2. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Bioinformatics, or Systems Biology
    3. Strong command of written and oral English
    4. Experience in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Python, or Matlab will be advantageous
    5. Detail-oriented, highly motivated, proactive, and passionate about research with the ability to work independently
    Benefits:
    1. Monthly stipend provided - 3 years for Ph.D. and 2 years for Master's
    2. Collaborative projects with local and international peers
    3. Research will be conducted at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Big Data (AIBIG), UMK Kampus Kota, Kota Bharu Kelantan
    4. Candidates will be registered under the Faculty of Data Science and Computing (FSD), UMK Kampus Kota, Kota Bharu Kelantan
    How to Apply:
    1. Interested candidates may send their CV to akmal@umk.edu.my or marieanne@umk.edu.my before 6 August 2023.
    2. Potential candidates will be notified for an online interview.
    Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to advance your knowledge and contribute to groundbreaking research in the field of Artificial Intelligence. Apply now and take the first step towards a rewarding postgraduate journey!
