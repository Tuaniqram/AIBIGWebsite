About Us

Established in 2017, AIBIG is your AI and Big Data destination. We focus on research, tech programs, and digital innovation in areas like AI, Big Data, Data Science, and more.

In partnership with top tech giants like Microsoft, Alibaba, and Huawei, we're shaping the future of tech education to meet industry needs. Our diverse team of experts ensures a multidisciplinary approach.

AIBIG shapes academic programs and promotes IT and digital innovation through events, competitions, and training. Join us on our journey to create a unique and innovative tech hub.

Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT is a network of devices, machines, or even living beings with unique IDs that can exchange data over a network without needing direct human interaction.

Artificial Intelligence

AI refers to computer systems or robots that can perform tasks typically done by intelligent beings.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is about computer systems learning to perform tasks without explicit instructions, relying on patterns and inference.
