Our Vision

To become a global reference centre for research and innovation in the field of artificial intelligence and big data


Our Mission

To cultivate strong, ongoing relationships with stakeholders and parties of interest to empower communities, governments, and industries to achieve exceptional levels of productivity and excellence through innovative research, talent development, and the application of artificial intelligence and big data technology across various domains.



Our Objectives
  • To research and develop theory, technology and application of IR4.0 especially in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data.
  • To engage Artificial Intelligence and Big Data with industry players.
  • To produce technopreneur in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data.
  • To give training of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data for society.
